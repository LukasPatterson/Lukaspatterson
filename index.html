Bio

Southwest Virginia native Lukas Patterson fuses crushing riffs, dreamy shoegaze textures, and retro 80s synth tones into a sound that hits as hard as it haunts. Former lead guitarist of Star City Meltdown, Lukas toured with Korn, Skid Row, 10 Years and more. Now solo, he blends 7- and 8-string guitar work, slap techniques, and cinematic production into something bold and unapologetically unique.
